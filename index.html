About Me

Over 15 years shipping games across multiple platforms, including 4 years working internationally with large teams on award-winning projects. Post-graduated in Digital Game Development and Unity Certified Expert Programmer.

My expertise is in Unity and C#, with a particular strength in UI programming, an area where I've consistently delivered complex, polished systems across very different types of projects. I bring the experience of someone who has worked at every scale, from solo indie projects to global productions, and I know what it takes to deliver in both.
